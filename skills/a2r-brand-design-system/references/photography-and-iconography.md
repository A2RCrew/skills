# A2R Photography and Iconography

> Source: A2R Brandbook pp. 34, 50-51 (June 2025, The Branx Europe S.L.)

## Photography Style

The photographic direction for A2R focuses on:

- **Contemporary, clean spaces**: Modern, minimalist, well-designed environments reflecting innovation and trust
- **Natural and realistic ambiance**: Authentic photos showcasing collaboration and learning, highlighting human interaction and technology
- **Collaboration and creativity**: People in action, working together or individually, conveying the collaboration between AI and human creativity
- **Modern workspaces**: Professional and educational environments representing A2R's progressive approach
- **Subtle technology integration**: Technology shown discreetly, emphasizing AI benefits without overshadowing the human element

### Style Keywords

Clean, polished, approachable, professional, natural light, contemporary architecture.

## Display Icons

9 custom hand-drawn display icons taking visual cues from the brand's typeface (sharp, angular terminals). **Trajan-style** aesthetic.

**Important**: Display icons are NOT intended for UI use. They serve as expressive graphic elements reinforcing the brand's distinct, agentic, and research-driven identity.

## Fallback / Utility Icons

For UI contexts where custom display icons don't fit, use **Phosphor Icons**.

- **Source**: phosphoricons.com
- **Style**: Light version (recommended for sleek, modern look)
- **Sizes**: 16px, 24px, 32px
- **Usage**: UI elements, navigation, functional indicators
- **Color**: Pair with brand colors for consistency

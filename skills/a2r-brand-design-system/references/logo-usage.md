# A2R Logo Usage

> Source: A2R Brandbook pp. 9-17 (June 2025, The Branx Europe S.L.)

## Logo Components

The A2R logo is composed of geometric shapes representing A, 2, and R. It conveys simplicity, playfulness, and innovation, aligning with adaptive learning powered by AI.

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Symbol** | The isotype — geometric shapes forming A2R. Standalone icon. |
| **Wordmark** | The typographic "a2r" text. |
| **Logomark** | Symbol + Wordmark combined. The complete logo. |

## Logo Suite Hierarchy

| Variant | Priority | When to use |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Logomark** | Recommended (primary) | Default choice. Most recognizable representation. |
| **Symbol** | Recommended | Smaller touchpoints where space is limited. Highly recognizable. |
| **Wordmark** | Exceptional use only | Very specific situations where the full logo cannot be displayed. |

## Clear Space (Exclusion Zone)

The logo must be surrounded by an exclusion zone equal to the **width of the "r" character** in the wordmark. No other elements may intrude into this zone.

## Logo Color Versions

Two main color versions: **Solid Black** and **Silver Gray** (corporate colors).
Alternative pure versions: **Pure White** (`#FFFFFF`) and **Pure Black** (`#000000`) for situations where corporate colors are not appropriate.

### Logo on Brand Backgrounds

| Background | Logo Color | Asset variant |
|------------|-----------|---------------|
| Core Blue (`#2764F4`) | Silver Gray / White | `*_White` or `*_SolidBlack` (inverted) |
| Silver Gray (`#FBFCFD`) | Solid Black | `*_SolidBlack` |
| Solid Black (`#111218`) | Silver Gray / White | `*_White` |
| Fresh Green (`#A1E8C9`) | Solid Black | `*_SolidBlack` |
| Bright Yellow (`#F7FF8E`) | Solid Black | `*_SolidBlack` |
| Warm Orange (`#FFCDBF`) | Solid Black | `*_SolidBlack` |

### Logo on Backgrounds — Rules

**DO:**
- Maintain high contrast between logo and background
- Use on solid brand colors (see table above)
- Use White/Silver Gray logo on dark photos with overlay
- Use Solid Black logo on light photos with clear areas

**DON'T:**
- Place logo on low-contrast backgrounds (e.g., Silver Gray logo on light green)
- Place logo on busy photo areas without an overlay
- Use Solid Black logo on dark backgrounds without sufficient contrast

## Brand Icon

The brand icon (Symbol on Core Blue background, white symbol) is used for:
- Profile pictures (social media)
- App icons
- Favicons

Format: Blue (`#2764F4`) background with white symbol, designed to work within circles or squares.

## Co-Branding

When combining with a partner's logo:
- **Gap**: 0.25X between logos (X = width of A2R logo)
- **Separator**: Lowercase "x" inscribed in a 0.5X square, centered in the gap
- **Partner height constraint**: Partner logo must never exceed the height of the A2R logo (small overhanging elements may be optically adjusted)

## Open Questions

- **Minimum logo size**: The brand book does not specify an explicit minimum pixel or mm size for the logo. When in doubt, ensure the symbol remains clearly legible.
